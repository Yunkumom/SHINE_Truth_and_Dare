## Cultural safety / 文化安全

Prompts explicitly excluded sacred, ceremonial, clan, protected, dot-painting, and rock-art motifs. Indigenous Australian subjects use public/open story layers and landscape-led symbolism. The project does not claim cultural ownership or authority.

提示詞明確排除神聖、儀式、氏族、受保護、點畫與岩畫圖騰。澳洲原住民族相關主題只採公開層次與地景象徵；本專案不主張文化所有權或詮釋權威。

## Generated source files / 生成來源檔

Original PNG generations remain in the local Codex generated-images directory. Website copies are resized, metadata-free WebP files; the originals were not deleted. The shared prompt pattern was: “original vertical card illustration, dark navy and antique gold, engraved storybook realism, no text or logos, no copied artwork,” followed by each subject and its cultural exclusions.

原始 PNG 保留在本機 Codex generated-images 資料夾；網站使用縮放並移除中繼資料的 WebP 副本，原圖未刪除。共用提示詞方向為：「原創直式卡圖、深海軍藍與古金色、雕版故事書寫實、無文字與標誌、不複製既有作品」，再加上各主題與文化排除條件。
