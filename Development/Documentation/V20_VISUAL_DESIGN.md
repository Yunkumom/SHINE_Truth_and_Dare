# Encounter Cards v20 — v16 Visual Restoration

## Approved Direction

v20 restores the visual hierarchy and collectible-card quality of `encounter_cards_v16.html` without modifying immutable v15–v19 releases. The supplied v16 entrance screenshot is the primary composition reference, not merely a colour reference.

## Entrance Interface

- Preserve the 430 × 932 iPhone Pro Max canvas.
- Recreate the v16 header: circular star mark, 相遇卡 wordmark, small `ENCOUNTER CARDS · V20`, and compact 中／EN／中/EN switch.
- Recreate the large bilingual editorial headline, italic English subtitle, supporting copy, and fine gold rules on a textured navy background.
- Recreate the ivory setup sheet with numbered heading, two-column name/contact fields, L1–L5 selectors, three card-type selectors, and a full-width dark Begin bar.
- On narrow phones the composition may scroll inside the phone canvas when accessibility text scaling requires it; ordinary 430 × 932 rendering must fit without clipping.

## Game and Keepsake Cards

- Use the v16 mythic collectible-card structure: warm metallic outer frame, parchment header, dominant framed artwork, restrained foil/glint, and a separate parchment text panel.
- Never place a generic overlay over the deity artwork. Question and blessing panels remain structurally separate from the image.
- Each drawn encounter stores a question, an independently selected deity artwork, and a blessing. The three parts remain stable for that revealed card and its exported PNG.
- Every keepsake PNG includes a blessing in the selected language mode.

## Artwork Library

- v20 begins with 18 works: the nine preserved deity portraits plus one new pose/action variant for each deity.
- Taiwan-shaped details are hidden as discoverable motifs in garments, weapons, jewellery, embroidery, lanterns, temple carving, waves, mountains, maps, or background objects. They must feel integrated and need not appear in clouds.
- Artwork remains respectful, contains no text or watermark, and is recorded in the asset catalogue.

## Desktop Geometry

- The entire 430 × 932 shell scales uniformly to fit the visual viewport.
- The scaled shell is centered horizontally and vertically inside a wrapper whose physical dimensions equal the scaled result.
- Desktop acceptance sizes include 1920 × 826, 1440 × 900, 1280 × 720, and 1024 × 768. At each size the complete shell remains visible and centered.

## Privacy and Compatibility

- Preserve Chinese, English, bilingual modes; Levels 1–5; Truth, Dare, Surprise; pointer/keyboard draw; PNG/Web Share fallback; PWA/offline behavior; and memory-only sensitive fields.
- Do not add analytics, accounts, backend transmission, or persistent personal-data storage.
