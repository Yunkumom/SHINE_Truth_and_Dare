# Purpose / 專案目的

## Mission / 任務

Maintain Truth and Dare / Encounter Cards as a safe, bilingual, mobile-first conversation game that helps two people share a question, a story, and a memorable encounter.

維護 Truth and Dare／相遇卡，作為安全、雙語、行動裝置優先的對話遊戲，讓兩個人透過問題與故事創造值得記住的相遇。

## Audience / 服務對象

People meeting for the first time, acquaintances, friends, close partners, and SHINE AI OS maintainers evolving the product.

初次見面的人、熟人、朋友、親近關係，以及持續發展產品的 SHINE AI OS 維護者。

## In Scope / 範圍內

- Versioned Encounter Cards product artifacts and future modular source
- Chinese, English, and bilingual card experiences
- Levels 1–5, card modes, mobile gestures, export, and sharing
- Product governance, privacy, reconstruction, and verification documentation

## Out of Scope / 範圍外

- Hosting, Git publication, or deployment without explicit approval
- Accounts, analytics, backend transmission, or cloud storage of personal fields
- Unreviewed expansion of 18+ content or third-party artwork
- Claiming the v15 packaged artifact is the unavailable original TypeScript source
